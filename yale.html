Yale for Humanity

Yale's For Humanity campaign involved creating interactive kiosks and touring around the country with them. The kiosks were designed to provide information on the fundraiser campaign in an easily digestible way. As the sole programmer and point of contact with the client, I was responsible for programming the kiosks using Ventuz 6.12 and ensuring seamless operation with the custom hardware solution provided by my company on-site.

Responsibilities

  • Programming interactive kiosks using Ventuz 6.12
  • Collaborating with the client to understand their requirements and expectations
  • Ensuring seamless integration of software with the custom hardware solution
  • Touring with the kiosks and providing on-site support

Technologies Used

  • Ventuz 6.12 Designer
  • Custom hardware
  • Touchscreen Kiosks
