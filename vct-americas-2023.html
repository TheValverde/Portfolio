VCT Americas 2023

Collaborated with Riot Games as a Lead Programmer for the VCT Americas data-driven real-time graphics package. My primary responsibility was to develop dynamic, data-driven graphic templates that live operators could efficiently utilize during live broadcasts. I employed Ventuz 6.12 Designer to create the templates and ensured they were configured for seamless and user-friendly interaction with real-time data updates within the Ventuz Director environment.
