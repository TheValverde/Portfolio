Yale For Humanity

Yale's For Humanity campaign required me to create interactive Kiosks, and travel around the country touring with them. Kiosks were meant to provide information on the fundraiser campaign in an easily digestible way. My role was as the sole lead programmer and point of contact with client. The Kiosks were programmed using Ventuz 6.12, and ran off a custom hardware solution that my company provided on-site.

George Mason University

I worked with George Mason University to create a visual, touchscreen interactive solution for their fundraiser. Once again, I revisited the Kiosk workflow established earlier that year while working with Yale. I provided one interactive Kiosk to help donors navigate through all the campaigns that were being presented for that fundraiser. Once again, Ventuz 6.12 was used for the programming and the same custom hardware solution was used for on-site service.
