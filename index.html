Q-OSC | 2023

As a programmer, I was challenged to create a user-friendly solution for efficiently mapping OSC messages to specific keys and sending them to a designated server upon pressing the keys. Q-OSC, developed over a single weekend, became my first Python desktop application that was successfully delivered to the client. The application leveraged customtkinter for the frontend and pythonosc for the backend, employing a JSON data structure for storing settings. The client at Quince Imaging expressed immense satisfaction with the swift turnaround and the application's ease of use. To date, Q-OSC continues to receive updates and benefit from the addition of new features.
